Frequently Asked Questions

Does one account support multiple devices?
Our single-connection plans support one device at a time. For simultaneous viewing on multiple devices, check our Multi-Connection plans on the pricing page.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes! Contact us on WhatsApp and we can provide a short trial so you can verify quality before purchasing.
How does WhatsApp ordering work?
Simply tap "Get Package" or message us on WhatsApp. Tell us which plan you want and we will guide you through activation — typically done in under 5 minutes.
How long does delivery take?
Activation is instant — you receive your credentials within minutes of order confirmation.
What countries are included?
Our catalog includes channels and VOD from hundreds of countries worldwide including Europe, Middle East, North Africa, and more.
Will my subscription renew automatically?
No. Subscriptions do not renew automatically. You will be notified before expiry and can renew when convenient.

Yes! Check our Affiliate page. Invite a friend who subscribes for 12+ months and receive 1 free month added to your account.
